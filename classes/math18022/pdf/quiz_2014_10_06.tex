\itm Let $f(x,y)=\cos(3xy)$, and suppose that $x=4st$ and $y=\dfrac{1}{s}+\dfrac{1}{t}$. Find $\partial f/\partial s$ and $\partial f/\partial t$ (by whatever method you prefer).  

\vfill

\itm A cylindrical tree grows in such a way that its height increases at a rate of 1 meter per year and its diameter increases at a rate of $\frac{1}{10}$ meters per year. At what rate is the volume of the tree increasing when its height reaches 10 meters, assuming that its diameter is 1 meter at that time? Express your answer in terms of $\pi$ and in units of cubic meters per year.
